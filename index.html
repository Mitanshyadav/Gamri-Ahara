According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Gamri Ahara village is 097638.
Gamri Ahara village is located in Dungarpur Tehsil of Dungarpur district in Rajasthan, India.
It is situated 18km away from Dungarpur, which is both district & sub-district headquarter of Gamri Ahara village.
As per 2009 stats, Gamdi Ahara is the gram panchayat of Gamri Ahara village.

The total geographical area of village is 1321 hectares. Gamri Ahara has a total population of 5,185 peoples.
There are about 1,036 houses in Gamri Ahara village. Dungarpur is nearest town to Gamri Ahara for all major economic activities,
which is approximately 18km away.
